It Pays to Hold

$Chisai is a deflationary coin. This means the coin becomes scarece over time. All holders earn more Chisai Dog Coin that is automatically sent to your wallet. This little guy just needs to be loved and held!

Anti-Whale System

The anti-whale system prohibits anyone from holding more than 5 Trillion Chisai Doge Coins in their wallet. If a wallet holds a 5 Trillion coins, the wallet will not receive any redistribution taxes. This keeps investors safe from large dumps! If multiple wallets are used, the individual will at the very least have to pay more taxes by having to buy and/or sale multiple times. Don't be greedy!
